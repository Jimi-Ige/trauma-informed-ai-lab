# Contributing to TIAIL

Thank you for your interest in the Trauma Informed AI Lab. This guide covers how to contribute.

## Who can contribute

We welcome contributions from:

- Developers and engineers (AI/ML, compliance, privacy, security)
- AI ethicists and policy researchers
- Clinicians and social workers
- People with lived experience of the systems TIAIL studies
- Writers, designers, and documentarians

Technical and non-technical contributions are equally valued.

## How to contribute

1. Fork this repository.
2. Clone your fork locally.
3. Create a feature branch from `main`.
4. Make your changes.
5. Open a pull request against `main`.

Pull requests are reviewed for technical correctness, alignment with TIAIL's [ethical AI principles](EthicalAIPrinciples.md), and consistency with the institute's published voice: sentence-case headings, active voice, no marketing language.
